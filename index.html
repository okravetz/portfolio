Bio

Oren is a User Experience Designer with formal training and experience in user research, wireframing, interaction design, usability testing, and prototyping.

As an Experience Design Lead at Isobar, Oren has been responsible for independent project threads and for leading a team of junior designers. He has experience with every stage of the design process from user research and product definition through launch. These projects include responsive solutions that account for various screen sizes including desktop, tablet, phone, large monitors and TVs. Between projects, Oren presented an intro to VR design to the UX design community at Isobar and is actively involved in the NowLab.

Oren's experience at Isobar includes designing websites, mobile apps, desktop applications, and large-scale web applications for clients such as Bloomberg, Sotheby’s, LPL Financial, MSCI, D. E. Shaw, NBCUniversal, Google, Umpqua Bank, The Wall Street Journal, IntegraMed, the U.S. Air Force, General Motors, Altar’d State, Flexport, Baker Hughes, Cisco, AllianceBernstein, American Express, and Olive Garden.

Oren's other interests include visual and creative design—especially photography and typography—and working the latest cutting-edge technologies such as wearables, smart things, and VR/AR/MR experiences.

In 2008, Oren received his B.S. in Software Engineering from Washington University in St. Louis and in 2010, his Masters in Human-Computer Interaction from Carnegie Mellon University.
