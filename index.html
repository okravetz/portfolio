About

Hey! I'm Oren (he/him/his)—a product design leader, husband, and father of two exceedingly cute gingers.

I've spent 15+ years designing intuitive digital experiences across healthcare, financial services, e-commerce, and enterprise SaaS. I love the challenge of turning complex problems into simple, delightful solutions—whether that's building tools that professionals rely on daily, designing data-heavy dashboards that make sense, or finally replacing that thing your users are still doing in Excel.

I've led cross-functional teams at agencies like Merkle, Dentsu Creative, and Isobar, working with clients ranging from American Express and Bloomberg to Anthem and the U.S. Air Force. I've launched new products from scratch, established design systems for enterprise clients, mentored designers, and led research and workshops that have driven millions in business impact. My approach combines deep user research, strategic thinking, and hands-on design—from wireframes and prototypes to high-fidelity UI.

Outside of work, I play video games with my kids, catch Broadway shows when I can, play D&D, take photos, mix cocktails, and occasionally brew beer. I'm also involved in my local community, volunteering with organizations focused on building welcoming, inclusive spaces.

I'm always excited to meet new people and tackle interesting problems. Let's work together to make the digital world a little bit better—one app at a time!
